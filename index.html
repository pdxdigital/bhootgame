Privacy Policy for Pdx bhoot Game

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

Introduction

PDX Digital Team ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Pdx Bhoot Game mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Service.

Your privacy is critically important to us. Our guiding principle is to collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to make our game work and to improve your experience.

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent about the data we DO NOT collect. We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes:

  • Your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.
  • Your device's unique identifiers (like Advertising ID or Android ID).
  • Your location data (GPS or network-based).
  • Access to your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Information We Collect (Non-Personal Data)

The only data our game handles is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. This data is purely for gameplay functionality:

  • Game State Data: Information like your score, survival time, and game progress is saved on your device's local storage. This allows you to continue your game and see your stats. This data never leaves your device.
  • Game Settings: Your selected language preference is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Our Service may use third-party services to provide a better experience. These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

  • Google AdMob & AdSense: Used for showing advertisements in the game.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used for collecting anonymous usage data to improve the game.
    Firebase Analytics Policy

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. In the game, we explicitly state it is not suitable for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with any information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email:
